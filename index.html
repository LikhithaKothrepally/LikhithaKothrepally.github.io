About me

Hi, I'm Likhitha Kothrepally, a passionate Data Science enthusiast with a strong academic background. I earned my Masters in Data Science from the University at Buffalo, where I explored programming languages and database management systems, honing my technical skills along the way.

With a solid foundation from my Masters in Data Science, I am deeply passionate about engaging in Data Analyst projects. Proficient in Python, SQL, Tableau, and R, I have successfully executed projects that involve comprehensive data analysis and visualization. My interests have expanded to encompass Data Engineering technologies, including Apache Spark, Hadoop, Airflow, Kafka, and concepts related to Data Warehousing.

In my one-year experience in Software Development at Accenture, I specialized as an Azure DevOps expert, gaining valuable insights into azure services and CI/CD pipeline implementation. This professional journey has motivated me to explore Cloud Technologies further, actively learning and implementing solutions using AWS and Azure platforms.
